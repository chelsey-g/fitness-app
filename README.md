Features
BMI & Calorie Calculators: Calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI) and determine your daily calorie needs.
Goal Setting: Set and track your personal fitness goals.
Friendly Competition: Create challenges and compete with friends to stay motivated.
Personalized Workouts: Design custom workout routines tailored to your fitness objectives.
Installation
To run HabitKick.app locally, follow these steps:

<h2>Clone the repository:</h2>
<code>git clone https://github.com/your-username/habitkick.app.git
cd habitkick.app
</code>

<h2>Install dependencies:</h2>


<code>npm install</code>

<h2>Start the development server:</h2>


<code>npm start</code>

<h2>Open your browser:</h2>

Navigate to http://localhost:3000 to see the application in action.

<h2>Usage</h2>
<h3>BMI & Calorie Calculators</h3>
<li><b>Calculate BMI:</b> Enter your height and weight to calculate your BMI.
<li><b>Calorie Needs:</b> Determine your daily calorie requirements based on your activity level.
<h3>Goal Settings</h3>
<li><b>Set a goal:</b> Click on "Start a Goal" and fill in your fitness objectives and deadlines.
<li><b>Track progress:</b> Monitor your goal completion status regularly.
<h3>Friendly Competition</h3>
<li><b>Create a competition:</b> Challenge your friends to a fitness competition.
<li><b>Track competition:</b> See how you rank among your friends and stay motivated.
<h3>Personalized Workouts</h3>
<li><b>Create a workout:</b> Design a workout plan that suits your fitness level and goals.
<li><b>Follow routines:</b> Stick to your personalized workout routine to achieve better results.
